1. Introduction

Welcome to Hako: AI Flashcard Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information necessary to provide you with our services.

Account Information:

When you sign in using Google, we collect your Name and Email Address to create your account and save your flashcards.

User-Generated Content:

We store the flashcards, decks, and input prompts you create to provide the service across your devices.

Payment Information:

We do not store your financial information (like credit card numbers) on our servers. All payments are processed securely by our third-party payment processor, Stripe.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service: To create your account, log you in, and allow you to generate and save flashcards.
  • To process payments: To verify your subscription status via Stripe.
  • To generate AI content: To process your input topics or text through Google Gemini to create flashcards automatically.
  • To communicate with you: To send you important updates regarding your account or changes to our policies (we do not send spam).

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to help us operate our app. These parties access your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Google Sign-In (Authentication)

We use Google Sign-In to secure your account. By using this service, you authorize us to access your Google profile information (Name and Email) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Gemini (AI Generation)

We use Google Gemini to provide our AI flashcard generation features. When you request to generate cards, the text or topics you provide are sent to Google's AI models for processing. We advise users not to include sensitive personal or confidential information in their AI generation prompts.

Stripe (Payments)

Payment processing services are provided by Stripe. We do not collect or store your payment card details. That information is provided directly to Stripe, whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy. You can read Stripe's Privacy Policy here: https://stripe.com/privacy

We will retain your Name, Email, and User Content only for as long as is necessary to provide you with our services. If you choose to delete your account, this information will be permanently removed from our database.
